Court Case Monitoring System (CCMS) aims to facilitate departments, advocates, HODs, LR and Secretaries to manage and monitor court cases pertaining to the Departments of Chandigarh Administration.

CCMS is integrated with the National Judicial Data Grid using the NAPIX platform for District and High Court cases. CAT cases are also integrated, while Supreme Court integration is under process.

CCMS operates on a role-based system ensuring users view and perform actions relevant to them. Case details are automatically fetched and notified by the Law Department.

Android and iOS mobile applications are also available.
